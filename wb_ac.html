Same Pair Comparison

The graph below shows the minutiae mappings of the latent print M010003, across many examiners. The complete black dots show the (x, y) coordinates that examiners marked on the latent print during the analysis stage, and the slightly transparent points show the (x, y) coordinates that examiners marked on the latent print during the comparison stage. The lines indicate movements of the minutiae markings done by examiners between the analysis and comparison stages. The hex shapes show the density of clusters of points in the comparison stage; darker hexes show a larger cluster of points and lighter hexes show a smaller cluster of points.

ggplot(data = m010003_ac) +
  geom_hex(aes(x = x_comparison, y = y_comparison, alpha = log(..count..)),
           bins = 20, fill = "#000000") +
  geom_point(aes(x = x_analysis, y = y_analysis), alpha = 0.5) +
  geom_point(aes(x = x_comparison, y = y_comparison)) +
  geom_segment(aes(x = x_analysis, y = y_analysis, xend = x_comparison,
                   yend = y_comparison), alpha = 0.5) +
  my_theme +
  scale_color_grey() +
  labs(title = "Pair M010003: Analysis Evaluation", subtitle = "Sherry Huang, July 2021",
       x = "x", y = "y", color = "Analysis Type")
